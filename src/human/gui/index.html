Thank you for participating to our research: Generating False-Positive Test Cases for Voice Recognition System. In each step, you will hear a voice, only once. Then, you will be asked two questions:
1. Could you Comprehend the voice?
2. What was it?
Based on your answers, we will determine the comprehensibility of the voice. You may notice some phrases used in a voice are similar to others. Please, DO forget the previous answers and treat each voice independently, and memorylessly. So there should not be cases where you say, "Oh, I think it makes sense for this voice to mean that."
Thank you again for your participation. Test cases will involve 20 voices, each of which takes just a few seconds to complete. Click the button below to begin.
